    HERE'S HOW TO PLAY

    This ticket will feature three game plays. Remove the latex covering the play area to reveal ten WINNING NUMBERS and thirty YOUR NUMBERS, with a prize amount beside each of the YOUR NUMBERS play symbols. If any of the WINNING NUMBERS match any of the YOUR NUMBERS, win the prize amount shown to the right of the matched number(s). If a "BOLT" symbol is revealed in any of the YOUR NUMBERS play spots, automatically win the PRIZE shown for that symbol.

    POWER SPOT: Remove the latex covering the four POWER SPOTS. If a "200" or "500" symbol in any of the four POWER SPOTS is revealed, win $200 or $500 instantly!

    POWER UP MULTIPLIER: Remove the latex covering the POWER UP MULTIPLIER. Reveal a "5X", "10X", "20X", or "50X" symbol, multiply all wins on ticket by that amount.

    MORE THAN $171 MILLION IN PRIZES!

    Approximately 9.12 million POWER 50X tickets are initially planned in this game. The New Jersey Lottery reserves the right to subsequently increase this quantity of tickets. Should additional tickets be introduced, prize levels and frequency of winning will be consistent with the initial quantity of tickets. In the POWER 50X Scratch-Offs game, New Jersey allocates approximately 70% of the gross receipts, net of free tickets, to prizes. On the average, better than 1 ticket in 4 wins a prize. Odds and number of winners may vary based on sales, distribution and claims.

    *The $3,000,000 prize will be paid in 25 annual gross installments of $120,000 each. At the time the prize is claimed, the Claimant shall be entitled to change the election to a Cash Option where the winner shall receive a payment equal to the gross cash value of $2,090,300 provided, however, that no change to the election shall be permitted after payment of a cash prize, or after payment of the first installment of an annuity prize, or subsequent to the sixtieth (60th) day following the date at which the prize was claimed.
